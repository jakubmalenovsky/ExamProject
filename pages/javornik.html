This scenic hike from Rožnov pod Radhoštěm to Javorník Mountain offers stunning views, dense forests, and peaceful landscapes. While the trip is challenging due to its distance, it can be shortened by taking a bus closer to Dolní Paseky, making the hike more manageable. The trail features moderate ascents and rest areas, making it suitable for families and casual hikers looking for a rewarding outdoor experience. After reaching Javorník, you can enjoy the beautiful surroundings before heading back to Rožnov pod Radhoštěm.

Restaurants

On the trail from Rožnov pod Radhoštěm to Javorník Mountain, you'll find a couple of great spots to stop for a meal. The first is Koliba na Pasekách in Dolní Paseky, a charming restaurant offering traditional Czech cuisine in a cozy, rustic setting—perfect for a relaxing break.
The second option is at the top of Javorník Mountain, where you'll find a restaurant with stunning views of the surrounding landscape, offering a great place to rest and enjoy a hearty meal before heading back.

Transport

To reach Dolní Paseky from Rožnov pod Radhoštěm, you can take a bus that brings you closer to the starting point of your hike. Buses from the center of Rožnov regularly connect to this area, providing a convenient option to shorten your journey before beginning your hike. Once you reach Dolní Paseky, you can easily continue on foot toward Javorník Mountain and beyond.

Equipment

In winter, it's important to be well-prepared for the hike. Make sure to bring really warm clothing, including thermal layers, a good winter jacket, gloves, and a hat to protect yourself from the cold. Since weather conditions in the mountains can be extreme, it's advisable to check the forecast before heading out. Additionally, if you're hiking in deep winter, crampons are highly recommended to ensure stability on icy or snowy sections of the trail. Sturdy, waterproof boots are also essential for navigating wet or slippery paths.
