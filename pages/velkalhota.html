Link

This easy and family-friendly trail connects Hlaváčky to the iconic tree near Velká Lhota and continues to Střítež nad Bečvou. The route offers beautiful views, peaceful landscapes, and lush greenery, making it ideal for families with children. With gentle inclines, well-maintained paths, and plenty of rest spots, this leisurely track ensures a relaxing outdoor experience for all skill levels. Perfect for those seeking a calm and scenic hike through nature.

Restaurants

If you're looking for a place to stop along the trail, Obecní dům in Velká Lhota is an option. While it's not anything spectacular, it's decent and offers traditional Czech food. It's a simple spot to take a break and grab a meal before continuing your hike.

Transport

For those traveling to the trail, you can easily reach Hlaváčky from Rožnov pod Radhoštěm by public transport. Buses run regularly between the two locations, offering a convenient way to start your hike. After completing the trail and reaching Střítež, you can take a bus back to Rožnov pod Radhoštěm, making the return journey just as easy. Public transport options are well-connected, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free trip.

Equipment

For this easy and family-friendly trail, no specialized equipment is needed. Comfortable walking shoes or light hiking boots are recommended, as the path is mostly gentle with well-maintained surfaces. If you're hiking with children, be sure to bring snacks, water, and perhaps a small backpack to carry essentials. A hat, sunscreen, and light jacket are also advisable, depending on the weather. Enjoy the hike with minimal gear while staying prepared for a relaxing outdoor experience.
