This picturesque route takes you from Horní Bečva to Radhošť and onwards to Rožnov pod Radhoštěm, showcasing stunning mountain vistas, dense woodlands, and tranquil meadows. The trail is moderately challenging, with gradual ascents and well-marked paths, making it perfect for both avid hikers and families looking for a rewarding adventure. Along the way, you'll encounter rest areas and opportunities to soak in the breathtaking surroundings, including the iconic statue of Radegast and the historic Radhošť Chapel. An unforgettable journey through the heart of Beskydy’s natural beauty.
